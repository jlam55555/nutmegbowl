Kids Bowl Free

[Kids Bowl Free] is designed by bowling centers to give back to the community and provide a safe, secure, and fun way for kids to spend time this summer. KidsBowlFree.com

Sign Up

Follow the steps below to participate:

  1. Go to kidsbowlfree.com/nutmeg and sign up. It's free!
  2. Receive free bowling passes every week by email
  3. Print out passes and bring to Nutmeg Bowl to redee, tje [asses

Details

The Kids Bowl Free program:

  • Is a program designed "to give back to the community and provide a safe, secure, and fun way for kids to spend time this summer," according to their website.
  • Is only eligible for children 15 and under.
  • Allows two free games a day within the start and end dates.
  • Requires that the passes be printed and physically brought in in order to redeem.

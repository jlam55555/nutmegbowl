Directions

Address

Nutmeg Bowl is located at 802 Villa Avenue, Fairfield at the junction of Kings Highway and Villa Avenue. Find it on Google Maps.

Text Directions

From the Merritt Parkway North bound:
  1. Take exit 44
  2. At the end of the ramp, take a left
  3. At the next light, take a right onto Black Rock Turnpike
  4. Continue on Black Rock Turnpike until you come to the split of Black Rock Turnpike and Tunxis Hill
  5. Bear left onto Tunxis Hill
  6. At the third light turn right into the shopping center where Nutmeg Bowl is located
From the Merritt Parkway South bound:
  1. Take exit 44
  2. At the light at the end of the ramp take a left
  3. At the next light take a left onto Black Rock Turnpike
  4. Continue on Black Rock Turnpike until you come to the split of Black Rock Turnpike and Tunxis Hill
  5. Bear left onto Tunxis Hill
  6. At the third light turn right into the shopping center where Nutmeg Bowl is located
From I-95 North bound:
  1. Take exit 24
  2. At the end of the ramp take a left
  3. Go under I-95 and bear right onto Kings Highway
  4. Nutmeg Bowl is approximately one mile on your left
  5. At the junction of Kings Highway and Villa Avenue
From I-95 South bound:
  1. Take exit 24
  2. At the end of the ramp take a right
  3. At the next light take another right onto Kings Highway
  4. Nutmeg Bowl is approximately 3/4 of a mile on your left
  5. At the junction of Kings Highway and Villa Avenue
